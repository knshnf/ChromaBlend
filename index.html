Monge-Kantorovitch Linear Color Transfer

Derived from the theory of mass transportation, the Monge-Kantorovitch Linear Color Transfer algorithm offers an optimal solution for image color transfer.

Optimal

Minimizes the amount of color changes to ensure integrity of the original image remain intact.

Intuitive

Maintains the brightness and darkness levels of pixels.

Efficient

Provides a better mapping for a similar computational complexity to the popular methods.

Consistent

Produces relatively similar results under different colour spaces.
